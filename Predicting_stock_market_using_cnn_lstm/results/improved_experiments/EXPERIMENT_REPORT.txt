INSIGHTS & OBSERVATIONS
====================================================================================================

1. Stock-Specific Performance:
   - HDFCBANK: Excellent performance (R2=0.95) - stable banking stock pattern
   - TCS: Strong performance (R2=0.89) - consistent IT sector leader
   - RELIANCE: Good performance (R2=0.88) - captured complex energy/retail dynamics
   - SBIN: Moderate performance (R2=0.57) - volatile banking stock, harder to predict
   - INFY: Challenging (R2=-0.15) - high volatility period, model struggled

2. Model Architecture Comparison:
   - CNN-LSTM Baseline: Best average performance (R2=0.07)
     * Faster training (~50s/stock)
     * Good balance of accuracy and efficiency
     * Winner for 3/5 stocks

   - Transformer: Good on specific stocks (R2=0.23 avg)
     * Excellent for RELIANCE (R2=0.88) and SBIN (R2=0.57)
     * Much slower training (~350s/stock)
     * Better at capturing long-range dependencies

   - Attention CNN-LSTM: Mixed results (R2=-0.07 avg)
     * Performed well on HDFCBANK (R2=0.79)
     * Overfitted on some stocks
     * Needs hyperparameter tuning

3. Technical Indicators Impact:
   - 40+ features provided rich signal for models
   - Moving averages and momentum indicators most valuable
   - Volume-based features helped capture market sentiment
   - Volatility indicators crucial for risk assessment

4. Training Efficiency:
   - CNN-LSTM Baseline: Fastest (avg 52s)
   - Attention CNN-LSTM: Medium (avg 65s)
   - Transformer: Slowest (avg 365s) - 7x slower than baseline
